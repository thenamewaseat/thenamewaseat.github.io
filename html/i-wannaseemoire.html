Ideas

We hoped to create a microscopic scene of an undersea creature – an AI robotic sea turtle, living in the year 2050. At this time, almost every sea creature is facing extinction (Pavid, n.d.). Humans are still using paper straws, even produced biometric-animals with paper straws that cannot even survive in the sea – they will be soften just like when we are drinking sodas with paper straws, and we still want to feed them with paper straws… Sea turtles are also smoking with paper straws…
