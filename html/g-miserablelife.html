Miserable Life


Project Details

Tags:

Game Design, Godot

Type:

Group Project (3 People)

Role:

Level Designer, Script Writer, UI prototype


Overview

The background story of our game is about the nightmare of the mouse. He can’t stand the stress from human life. He then tried to escape its original workplace from the human village. However, humans set a lot of traps to prevent the mouse escaping. So, you need to try to lead the house to escape this stressful world.
