The World of Foley Artist –
An Essential Role of Post-Production in Filmmaking

Project Details

Tags:

Installation, Makey Makey, Unity

Type:

Group Project (6 People)

Role:

Circuit Designer, Game Mode Developer


Gallery

Ideas

Foley Artist – a special and rare job from the post-production occupations as our primary idea objective, and we hope to bring out a message through an interactive installation that we should provide respect for postproduction roles in filmmaking industry, letting audience to acknowledge more about foley artist and post-production process through playing an interactive exploration game in our installation with immersive decoration setup.
