Overview

Isle BabaYaga is a third-person action-adventure game set on an uninhabited island during the midnight hours. Players assume the role of a worker assigned to the island for tree-cutting operations. However, upon waking, You, the protagonist, discovers that all fellow workers have mysteriously disappeared without a trace. The game challenges players to explore the island, investigate the circumstances surrounding the disappearance, and uncover the hidden truth.
