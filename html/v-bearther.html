What's the "Bear"ther today?


Showcase


Ideas

“An anthropomorphic polar bear living at snowy environments is the protagonist of the weather forecast. The changes in weather would affect his appearance, undergoing skin transformation dramatically, which is melting a polar bear skin from white to brown. The animation would end with a mysterious open-ending, such storytelling technique is expected to impress the general audience and convey them the weather information seamlessly. The bear also served as audience’s emotional projection for resonance, which is helpful for audience to aware of today’s weather while ensuring the forecast is attractive and memorable for all walks of lives. ”
